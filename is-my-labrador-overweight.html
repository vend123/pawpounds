Is my Labrador overweight?

If your Lab looks a little generous lately, you're not imagining it — and there's a real genetic reason behind those pleading dinner-time eyes.

Labradors sit near the top of every overweight-breed ranking vets publish, with an estimated 40–60% carrying excess weight. The good news: a Lab's ideal weight is well defined and the fixes are simple.

Ideal weight

Healthy adult Labrador weight
SexIdeal range
Male29–36 kg (65–80 lb)
Female25–32 kg (55–70 lb)

The Labrador gene, for real. Around a quarter of Labradors carry a deletion in a gene called POMC that blunts the feeling of fullness and ramps up food drive. It's why Labs are famously food-obsessed — and why portion discipline matters more for them than almost any breed.

How to tell if your pet is overweight

  • Feel for the ribs. Lay both hands flat on the ribcage; you should feel ribs easily under a thin layer without pressing.
  • Look from above. There should be a clear waist tucking in behind the ribs; a straight or bulging outline means no waist.
  • Check the side profile. The belly should tuck up behind the ribcage rather than hang level.

What to do about it

  • Weigh the food, don't scoop it. Eyeballing portions is the most common cause of overfeeding.
  • Count treats as calories — keep them under 10% of daily intake; swap biscuits for carrot or green bean.
  • Build up daily activity gradually, switching to low-impact options like swimming if joints are sore.
  • Ignore the eyes. That heartbreaking stare is the food-drive gene talking, not real hunger.
  • Reweigh monthly. Safe loss is about 1–2% of body weight per week; involve your vet for an overweight pet.

Frequently asked

How much should a Labrador weigh?

Healthy adult males are 29–36 kg (65–80 lb), females 25–32 kg (55–70 lb). Use body-shape checks to confirm.

Why is my Labrador always hungry?

About a quarter carry a POMC gene variant that dampens fullness and boosts food drive, so the hunger is partly wired in.

Is my Lab fat or big-boned?

Feel the ribs: easy to find with a visible waist means it's frame; buried ribs and no waist mean weight.
