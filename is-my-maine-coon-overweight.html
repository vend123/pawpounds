Is my Maine Coon overweight?

Maine Coons are supposed to be big — which makes telling healthy size from actual fat genuinely tricky.

Maine Coons are among the largest domestic cats, with big frames and coats to match, and they mature slowly — up to four or five years. All of that makes weight hard to judge by numbers alone.

Ideal weight

Healthy adult Maine Coon weight
SexIdeal range
Male5.9–8.2 kg (13–18 lb)
Female4.5–6.8 kg (10–15 lb)

Big frame vs. big belly. A Maine Coon can healthily weigh what would be obese in another cat, so the scale misleads. The tell is feel, not size: run your hands over the ribs and check for a waist. A large but lean Coon has findable ribs and a defined shape; a fat one is rounded with buried ribs. They also have the normal primordial belly pouch.

How to tell if your pet is overweight

  • Feel for the ribs. Lay both hands flat on the ribcage; you should feel ribs easily under a thin layer without pressing.
  • Look from above. There should be a clear waist tucking in behind the ribs; a straight or bulging outline means no waist.
  • Check the side profile. The belly should tuck up behind the ribcage rather than hang level.

What to do about it

  • Weigh the food, don't scoop it. Eyeballing portions is the most common cause of overfeeding.
  • Count treats as calories — keep them under 10% of daily intake; swap biscuits for carrot or green bean.
  • Build up daily activity gradually, switching to low-impact options like swimming if joints are sore.
  • Go slow with cats. Rapid loss risks fatty liver disease — involve your vet.
  • Reweigh monthly. Safe loss is about 1–2% of body weight per week; involve your vet for an overweight pet.

Frequently asked

How much should a Maine Coon weigh?

Males 5.9–8.2 kg (13–18 lb), females 4.5–6.8 kg (10–15 lb); some larger.

Is my Maine Coon big or fat?

Feel for ribs and a waist rather than reading the scale — a lean Coon is large but defined.

Why is my Maine Coon still growing?

They mature slowly, often up to 4–5 years, so young Coons keep filling out.
