Check it yourself in 10 seconds

You don't need a scale to spot trouble. Vets use three quick hands-on tests — try them on your pet right now.

The rib test

Can you feel the ribs?

Run your hands along the side. You should feel ribs easily under a thin layer, like the back of your hand. If you have to press, there's padding to lose.

The top-down look

Is there a waist?

Look down from above while they stand. A healthy pet narrows behind the ribs. A straight sausage — or bulging outward — means no waist.

The side view

Does the belly tuck up?

From the side, the tummy should tuck upward behind the ribcage. A belly that hangs level or sags is a classic overweight sign.
